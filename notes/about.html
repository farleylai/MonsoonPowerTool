Farley Lai is a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Iowa. He received the M.S. degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering in 2004 from the National Central University in Taiwan with a thesis proving a theorem regarding the fairness in distributed scheduling. Before coming to the University of Iowa, he had been working in the surveillance industry and led a project of developing video management software. His current research focuses on improving the performance and resource efficiency of mobile sensing applications through program analysis.

An up-to-date resume is available for reference. He is reachable via social networks.
